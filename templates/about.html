Using AVE

Here are instructions on setting up and using AVE.

Setting Up

For information on setting up AVE in your own computer, refer to the README in the GitHub.

Uploading a Video

Here is how to upload a video.

  1. Navigate to the Upload Video Form in the main section of the Homepage.
  2. Click the browse button to open up your File Explorer.
  3. Select your desired video file. **Editor currently only accepts .mp4 files.**
  4. Navigate to and click the Upload Video Button under the form.
  5. The page will now be updated with your video and video playback features!

Trimming a Video

Here is how to trim a video.

  1. Traverse to your specified timestamp and press the Update Marker 1 button. Anything before Marker 1 will be removed.
  2. Traverse to a later timestamp and press the Update Marker 2 button. Anything after Marker 2 will be removed.
  3. Press the Trim button.
  4. The video will be updated with the remaining section of the video.

Downloading a Video

Here is how to download your video once edited.

  1. Click the download button at the bottom of the page or use the keyboard shortcut ctrl+s.
  2. Your video download will begin. Depending on your browser setup, the video may automatically download or you may be prompted to confirm the download.

Keyboard Shortcuts

AVE comes equipped with keyboard shortcuts to help streamline your video editing.

Keyboard Shortcuts
ShortcutAction
P Play and pause video
J Move back 10 seconds in video
K Move back 1 second in video
L Move forwards 1 second in video
; Move forwards 10 seconds in video
Ctrl + S Download the video
Shift + Esc Emergency shut off for keyboard shortcuts
